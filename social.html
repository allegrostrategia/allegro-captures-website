Why retainer

DIY content isn't good enough anymore.
It just isn't.

In the online space as it is now, if you want to stand out you need professional photography and videography, consistently, not once a year at a brand shoot.

The other problem with DIY is consistency. Everyone falls off the wagon when they're responsible for their own content. When you're working with a team, you get quality and consistency.

That's what actually supports your sales.

01

A professional understands lighting, framing, sound. Your phone doesn't (and nor do you if you're being honest). People subconsciously notice the difference. The algorithm does too.
02

Every shoot starts with a content plan built around what you're selling next. Not content for content's sake. Content with the purpose of generating leads and sales.

Captions, scripts, editing, scheduling strategy. All included. You get content that's ready to post without lifting a finger.
