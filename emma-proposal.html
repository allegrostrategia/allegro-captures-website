Package 01

The Brand Voice Experience*

*We don't have your official name for this one yet, so we've gone with what we've been calling it in this proposal.

A half or full day with your client. You draw out their voice, we capture it properly. You handle the pre-work and the interviewing. We handle the cameras, the production, and the edit. They walk away with a real bank of clips, built to last a quarter.

The power comes from the edit. Because you're working conversationally rather than stopping and starting, we record the full session and pull everything out in post. It's more useful and it actually sounds like them.

The whole point of this is consistency. One day of filming gives your client enough content to show up every single week for three months, with room to spare. No scrambling for content, no one-off shoots that run dry by week two. They walk away sorted for a quarter.

A quick note on how we've priced this. Because you're bringing us in as part of your offer rather than working with us directly as a client, we've structured our fees on an agency model. That means a leaner margin so the numbers work inside your package pricing, not against it.

Suggested ongoing structure

Building this into a recurring offer

One shoot day works. But the real opportunity here is turning this into something your clients book on repeat, which also gives you some predictable monthly revenue rather than chasing one-off bookings.

A natural structure might look like this: a quarterly shoot with us, with a monthly 1-2-1 with you in between to work through what they're posting and make sure the content is landing. You do the monthly touchpoint, we handle the quarterly production. That combination gives your client accountability and output in the same package.

What this could include

A quarterly shoot day (half or full day depending on their needs) with us. Monthly 1-2-1 sessions with you between shoots. Content review and planning built into the rhythm so clips are always aligned to what they're selling. Structured as a quarterly investment for your client with everything mapped out in advance.

Why it works for you

Predictable income from a small roster of clients rather than constantly filling the calendar. Deeper results for your clients because the content has strategic direction behind it. And a cleaner story to tell when you're selling it: this is not a one-off shoot, this is an ongoing content partnership.
